# OWASP Juice Shop – Privacy Policy Challenge

## Category: Miscellaneous | Challenge Name: Privacy Policy | Difficulty: 1 Star

### Objective

- Read the privacy policy document inside the OWASP Juice Shop application.

### Challenge Description

- The task is simple: locate and open the **Privacy Policy** page/document from within the application.

## Prerequisites

- Access to OWASP Juice Shop
- Logged in account (any user account)

> If no account exists, create a dummy/test account.


## Step-by-Step Solution

### Step 1: Login

- Sign in using any valid account.

### Step 2: Open Account Menu

- Navigate to:

```text id="r1v6ep"
Account
````

### Step 3: Open Privacy Section

- Select:

```text
Privacy
```

### Step 4: Click Privacy Policy

- Open the link:

```text
Privacy Policy
```

## Result

- Once the policy page/document is opened, the challenge is marked as solved.


## Why This Challenge Exists

- This challenge teaches users to explore application content and legal/privacy sections that many testers ignore.

- Sometimes sensitive information, outdated statements, or hidden links can exist in these pages.

## Security Relevance

### Real-World Importance

- Privacy policy pages may reveal:
	- Data collection practices
	- Third-party services used
	- Contact emails
	- Company/legal details
	- Compliance gaps
	- Forgotten internal links

## Severity

**Informational**

## OWASP Mapping

- Information Gathering
    
- Reconnaissance
    
- Content Discovery

## Key Lesson

Not every challenge requires exploitation.  
Some vulnerabilities begin with careful observation and enumeration.

## Conclusion

The Privacy Policy challenge is a beginner-friendly task that reinforces the importance of exploring all visible pages, including legal and informational sections.
